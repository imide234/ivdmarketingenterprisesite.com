About IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE

IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE is a forward-thinking digital marketing agency established in 2021 to redefine the digital marketing landscape. Our mission is to illuminate every brand we work with—empowering businesses to grow, connect, and thrive through innovative digital strategies, marketing automation, and data-driven campaigns.

The company was founded by Imide Victor, a seasoned Digital Marketing Manager who has been promoting and building brands since 2019. With deep expertise in CRM management, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), social media marketing, content marketing, and website development, Victor envisioned a platform that would go beyond conventional marketing.

He asked a simple but powerful question:

What if we could create a marketing system that empowers businesses to achieve more—one capable of replacing traditional TV ads and giving brands complete confidence in their digital future?

That vision became IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE — a digital marketing enterprise designed not just to promote brands, but to transform them. We focus on empowering businesses, industries, and organizations by turning their goals and visions into reality through strategic marketing, brand visibility optimization, and performance-driven online advertising.

Based in Nigeria, IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE operates with one guiding principle:

“Your business goal is our goal and vision.”

We are dedicated to taking every brand to the next phase—building visibility, trust, and long-term growth through innovative marketing strategies, customer engagement, and sustainable digital transformation in an ever-evolving digital world.
